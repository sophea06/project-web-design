    Empowering Cambodian Youth Through Digital Education

    Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC) provides free IT training for underprivileged students.

    About Us

    Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC) is a non-profit organization founded in 2005 in Phnom Penh. We provide underprivileged youth with free, high-quality training in Information Technology, combined with English, soft skills, and personal development.

    Our Story

    • 2005 : PNC started in Phnom Penh, initially called CIST, with support from Accenture and Enfants du Mékong.

    • 2006 : Became an independent center under the name Passerelles Numériques Cambodia.

    • Today : Thousands of students have graduated, with more than 90% finding employment in IT companies, both in Cambodia and abroad.

    Our Values

    Trust

    We believe in honesty, integrity, and transparency in everything we do. Trust builds strong relationships between students, staff, and partners.

    Responsibility

    Every individual is responsible for their learning, actions, and future. We encourage accountability and leadership to create positive change.

    Solidarity

    We support and help each other, creating a strong and united community. Working together makes us stronger and more resilient.

    Respect

    We respect ourselves, others, and our environment. Diversity and equality are central to our learning community.

    At Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC), we believe that good nutrition is just as important as good education. That’s why we provide students with three healthy meals every day at our campus canteen.

    The canteen is more than just a place to eat — it is a space where students from different provinces share meals, build friendships, and strengthen solidarity.

    🍚 Nutritious food to keep students energized for studying and training.

    👩‍🍳 Hygienic and safe environment managed with care.

    🤝 Community spirit where students, staff, and volunteers come together.

    🏅 Sports at PNC

    At Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC), we believe that education goes beyond classrooms and computers. Sports play an important role in building a healthy, balanced, and united student life.

    💪 Health & Wellness – keeping students physically and mentally fit.

    🤝 Teamwork – learning how to cooperate and trust each other.

    🎉 Fun & Friendship – creating bonds outside of classrooms.

    Through sports, students develop discipline, solidarity, and respect — the same values that guide their studies and future careers.

    💃 Aerobics at PNC

    At Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC), we care about the health and well-being of our students. That’s why aerobic sessions are organized regularly to keep students active, energized, and motivated.

    🌟 Benefits of Aerobics at PNC

    💪 Stay Healthy – Improves stamina, flexibility, and overall fitness.

    😊 Reduce Stress – Helps students relax and balance study life.

    🎶 Fun & Energetic – Dancing and moving with music brings joy.

    By combining physical activity with music and rhythm, aerobic sessions strengthen both body and mind, helping students live healthier lives and perform better in their studies.
